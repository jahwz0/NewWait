1. Introduction

BLKBAR Ltd (“BLKBAR”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your personal information when you use our website, mobile application, and related services (collectively, the “Platform”).

BLKBAR is a UK-based digital platform that connects clients with independent hairstylists, braiders, barbers, and beauty professionals specialising in Afro and textured hair.

This Privacy Policy applies to:

  • Clients using the BLKBAR platform to discover and book services

By using our Platform, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
